Registration For Spaza Shops

As per the call from President Ramaphosa, all Spaza shops and food handling facilities must register with their local municipalities. As an owner, you are required to ensure that your shop is registered and complies with municipal by-laws, including food safety, zoning, building regulations, fire safety, and environmental laws.

  • Municipal Registration: Obtain a trading license/permit from the local municipality.
  • CIPC Registration: Register the shop as a legal entity with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC).

Note: Foreign nationals need a valid business visa. Health and safety compliance is crucial for all shops.
