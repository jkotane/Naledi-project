Health Compliance Checklist

Facility Hygiene

  • Action: Clean and repair any visible dirt, mold, or structural damage on surfaces.
  • Action: Replace damaged bins, ensure lids are functional, and set a schedule for waste disposal.

Food Storage and Handling

  • Action: Regularly monitor and record temperatures; service malfunctioning refrigeration units.
  • Action: Provide shelving or pallets to elevate goods from the floor.

Staff Hygiene

  • Action: Train staff on handwashing techniques, provide gloves and uniforms, and implement hygiene policies.
  • Action: Fix any broken stations and ensure they are stocked daily with necessary supplies.

Pest Control

  • Action: Hire a pest control service for inspection and treatment if pests are detected.
  • Action: Repair damaged seals, screens, or barriers to secure entry points.

Licensing and Documentation

  • Action: Renew expired permits and ensure they are visible in the facility.
  • Action: Establish a log system and assign staff responsibility for updates.
